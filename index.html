The Concept

The Community

Our Civic Suds community centers are built for busy families who want to knock out multiple birds with one stone.

The Vision

Civic Suds aims to turn clothes washing into a fun and productive activity for the whole family.

Our Story

Civic Suds is a new venture that builds upon the Wash & Learn Initiative (WALI) and the Laundry Literacy Coalition (LLC) initiatives and partnerships co-founded by Allister Chang.

Part laundromat, part community center, Civic Suds aims to equip guests with access to books, computers, and programming that provides actionable information and valued skills. Their ultimate goal is to transform the laundromat into a space for learning and growth for underserved families.
