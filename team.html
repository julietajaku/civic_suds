Our Team

Allister Chang

Co-Founder & CEO

Allister is a Fellow at Voqal and an Affiliate at Harvard University’s Berkman-Klein Center for Internet and Society. He holds a Master’s in Public Policy from the Harvard Kennedy School, and a B.A. in History from Tufts University. He was featured on stage at the closing plenary of the 2016 Clinton Global Initiative annual meeting, and he is on the International Literacy Association’s 2019 “30 Under 30” List.

Dennis Diaz

Co-Founder & COO

Dennis is a veteran of the laundromat industry. He sits on the Board of Directors of the Coin Laundry Association and is the president of New York City-based, SPYNR Inc., a digital marketing and software company that seeks to deliver a tangible return on investment for businesses that are seeking to improve their online presence and operations with technology.

Ryan Hafner

Co-Founder & CFO

Ryan has spent the majority of his career working on mergers and acquisitions, both as an institutional investor and an advisor to small business owners. Ryan holds a B.A. in economics from Carleton College where he was a member of the men's soccer and hockey teams.
